Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dishcovery?

Dishcovery is a community-driven website that allows users to share, explore, and rate recipes from all over the world. It's designed to highlight the cultural richness behind every dish.

Do I need an account to use Dishcovery?

You can browse recipes without an account, but to share recipes, rate them, or leave comments, you'll need to create a free account.

What makes Dishcovery different from other recipe websites?

Every recipe on Dishcovery comes with a cultural background or personal story, so you're not just learning how to cook, you're learning that dish matters to someone and where it comes from.

Can I upload my own recipes?

Yes! Once you're logged in, you can submit your own recipes along with photos, step-by-step instructions, and a personal or cultural story behind the dish.

How is content organized?

Recipes are categorized by cuisine, difficulty, meal type, and diet preferences, making it easy to discover new dishes tailored to your tastes or dietary needs.

How do ratings work?

Logged-in users can rate any recipe on a scale of 1-10, helping others find the most loved and trusted dishes in the community.

Is Dishcovery free to use?

Yes! Dishcovery is completely free to use and contribute to.

Can I interact with other users?

While direct messaging isn't available, you can engage with others by commenting on recipes, sharing feedback, and rating dishes.
